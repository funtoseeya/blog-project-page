About

We are excited to embark on a project to renovate and enhance our classrooms at Aurora Valley Schools. This page is dedicated to providing updates, engaging our community, and ensuring transparency throughout the project.

Milestones

  • Phase 1: Classroom assessment and planning
  • Phase 2: Renovation of classrooms in the West Wing
  • Phase 3: Renovation of classrooms in the East Wing
  • Phase 4: Final touches and finishing details
  • Phase 5: Grand reopening and celebration

FAQs

  • Q: When will the renovations begin?

    The renovations are scheduled to start in July 2023.

  • Q: How long will the entire project take?

    The estimated timeline for completion is one year.

  • Q: Will classes be disrupted during the renovation process?

    We have carefully planned the project to minimize disruptions to classes and ensure a smooth learning experience for our students.
